Greenplan Consultants is presenting a week long DesignBuilder training opportunity from the 15th - 19th October 2018 at 314 on Clark Guest House in Pretoria, Gauteng.

Topics covered

The course will cover the following topics on a day-by-day basis, please note that the first two days form a package called DesignBuilder fundamentals.

  • Day 1: 3D Modeller: The first module is the introduction to the DesignBuilder interface, with a focus on model geometry information input and drawing models.
  • Day 2: Simulation: This module focuses on running simulations and extracting data from DesignBuilder.
  • Day 3: SANS 10400-XA: This day demonstrates how DesignBuilder is easily used to determine whether a house complies with the SANS 10400-XA standard by following the building energy modelling route.
  • Day 4: Detailed HVAC: This module demonstrates how DesignBuilder is used to model modern HVAC systems at the component detail level.
  • Day 5: Advanced topics: This day looks at other powerful modules and calculation functionality within the software. The course is structured in such a way that the three most popular topics will be covered: (1) CFD (2) Natural Ventilation and (3) Thermal Comfort.

For more information on the topics covered during the course, please visit the Greenplan Consultants website.
